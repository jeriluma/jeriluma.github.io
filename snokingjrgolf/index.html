Completed

My father runs a non-profit organization: Sno-King Junior to help kids learn golf for free. I decided to help create a website for Sno-King Junior Golf. I collaborated with Leah Kim, who created the whole visual design. I was given high fidelity screenshots, and now I am currently recreating these screen shots into web pages. I am fully handling the coding of this project in HTML, CSS, and Javascript/JQuery.

Ongoing

My father wants to expand the Sno-King Junior Golf. He wants to add a way to share his golf lessons and materials to his students only, and be able to communicate with each student individually. To handle all this new content, I decided to utilize Wordpress as a content management system as well as a user management system. I am currently developing a custome wordpress theme to stay in consistent with the current theme. I am also developing a custom local navigation system per page.
